Dataplatform

Dataplatform is a service for every organization to unlock, store, manage and present data in an easy and secure way. Data platform is entirely based on open source software (including CKAN and Drupal). Data can be made available to all interested parties, but can also be shared only within your own organization or with partners. With Dataplatform you determine how and with whom you want to share your data. Civity is also one of the initiators of FIWARE Lab NL, a free development environment for data and Internet or Thing applications.
